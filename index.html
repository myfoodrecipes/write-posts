Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine or any type you like)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Parsley (optional, for garnish)

  7. Serve hot and garnish with parsley.

Tips for the Best Alfredo Pasta:

  • Add grilled chicken or shrimp for extra protein.
  • Use mozzarella with Parmesan for a cheesier flavor.
  • Add a bit of pasta water if the sauce gets too thick.
