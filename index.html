Easy Homemade Alfredo Pasta

Prep: 10 min • Cook: 15 min • Serves: 2–3

">

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine or any type you like)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until cooked (8–10 minutes).
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and sauté the garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add the heavy cream and stir on low heat.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese and mix until the sauce becomes creamy.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with parsley.

Tips for the Best Alfredo Pasta

  • Add grilled chicken or shrimp for extra protein.
  • Use mozzarella with Parmesan for a cheesier flavor.
  • Add a bit of pasta water if the sauce gets too thick.
