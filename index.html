Quick, creamy, and ready in 20 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine or any type you like)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until cooked (8‚Äì10 minutes).
  2. Melt butter and saut√© garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add the heavy cream and stir on low heat.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese until creamy.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Mix in the pasta and combine well.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with parsley.

Tips for the Best Alfredo Pasta:

  • Add grilled chicken or shrimp for extra protein.
  • Use mozzarella with Parmesan for a cheesier flavor.
  • Add a bit of pasta water if the sauce gets too thick.
